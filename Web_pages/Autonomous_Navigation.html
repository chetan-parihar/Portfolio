Overview

A collection of autonomous navigation projects demonstrating proficiency in SLAM, path planning, and sensor fusion across various robotic platforms including quadruped and wheeled robots.

  • Stack: ROS 2, Nav2
  • Sensors: 2D LiDAR, 3D LiDAR, Depth Cams
  • Algorithms: RTAB-MAP, SLAM
