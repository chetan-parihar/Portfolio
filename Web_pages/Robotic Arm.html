Details

Objective was to develop a prototype bot capable of efficiently lifting heavy weights within a warehouse environment. This bot will be controlled by human operators. Furthermore, we aim to equip the bot with line-following capabilities to aid in its navigation, thereby reducing the computational complexity associated with traditional navigation methods.

Unfortunately, that the project was not successful due to issues with faulty hardware and limited availability of hardware within the given timeframe.
